Snakes and Ladders

Snakes And Ladders

Snakes and Ladder is a modern twist on the classic board game, created in Unity with engaging 2D graphics and dynamic gameplay. Players roll the dice and navigate the board, but this version includes exciting power-ups that can help or hinder progress.

Snakes And Ladders Gameplay

Snakes And Ladders

Features.

1.Snake and Ladder Setup
Ladder: move the player ahead, skipping a number of squares.
Snakes: make the player slide back, losing progress and creating an element of unpredictability.
2.Power-ups
Power-ups are features that give players an advantage, enhancing their chances of winning or improving the game’s strategic depth. The Powerup like Super Six, Reverse Roll and X2.
3.Dice Roll System
The dice roll system is crucial part for the gameplay, as it determines the player's movement on the board.
4.Player Turn Management
Managing the turn-based system so each player has a fair chance to play.

Royal Bite

Royal Bite

Royal Bite is an immersive game where players take on the role of a restaurant manager, tasked with fulfilling various customer food orders. In this dynamic 3D environment, players must quickly prepare dishes, serve them to customers, and handle payments—all while managing time
and resources efficiently.

Royal Bite Gameplay

Royal Bite

Features.

1. Nav-Mesh Based Player Movement
Game use Unity's Nav-Mesh, which allows the player to navigate the restaurant with ease. The key feature here is the use of markers placed within the restaurant to define the paths the player can take.

2.Order Management System
The order management system is central to the gameplay, allowing the player to handle incoming customer orders, prepare meals, and serve them to customers in a timely manner.

3.Food Ingredient Purchase System
To maintain an efficient restaurant, players need to purchase ingredients to keep the kitchen stocked and ready to prepare meals. The ingredient purchase system allows players to manage resources and ensure they have the necessary ingredients for customer orders.

Ping Pong

Ping Pong

The Ping Pong game is a classic two-player simulation where each player controls a vertical paddle and tries to hit a ball back and forth. The ball bounces off paddles and walls, and players aim to score by getting the ball past their opponent's paddle. The game follows a 3-time goal system, where the first player to reach 3 points wins.

Ping Pong Gameplay

Ping Pong

Features.

1.Paddle System :  Two paddles for each player, with vertical movement along the screen.

2.Ball movement :  Ball movement with realistic bouncing and direction changes based on paddle interaction.

3.Goal system : A 3-time goal system where players compete to score goals by getting the ball past their opponent.

Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe

Tic-Tac-Toe is a classic strategy game where two players take turns to mark X s and O s on a 3x3 grid. The objective is to get three of your marks in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, before your opponent does. Your Unity version includes two modes: two-player mode for playing with a friend, and AI mode for playing against a computer opponent.

Tic Tac Toe Gameplay

Tic Tac Toe

Features.

1. Two-Player Mode:
Players take turns to place either an X or an O in one of the 9 available grid cells. The game alternates between Player 1 and Player 2, with Player 1 always starting. The grid updates visually with each move, showing Xs and Os in the selected cell.

2.AI Mode:
In this mode, the player faces off against an AI that takes the role of Player 2. The player still takes the first turn as Player 1, while the AI automatically takes the second turn as Player 2.

3. Game Flow:
Players (or the AI) select their marks (X or O) before starting the game. A restart option is available after a game is over (whether it ends in a win or a draw), allowing players to play again.
