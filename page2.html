Zombie Hunt

Zombie Hunt 

Zombie Hunt is an action-packed game where players take on the role of a soldier tasked with completing high-stakes missions in a world over run by zombies.

Zombie Hunt Gameplay

Zombie Hunt

Features.

1.Game Setup with C++ and Blueprint
Game contain a mix use of C++ and Blueprints, which is a great way to leverage both the performance benefits of C++ and the flexibility of Blueprints for visual scripting.

2.Landscape and Scenario Setup
The Game contain a beautiful and immersive landscape by using terrain tools to sculpt and paint the landscape, along with assets like trees, buildings, and other structures.

3.Car Driving System
A simple car driving system in Unreal Engine will add an exciting dynamic to the game, enabling players to drive vehicles for faster navigation across the map.

4.Enemy Zombie System with AI Navigation
Zombies are driven by AI navigation, which is crucial for a survival game. Zombie Feature : Patrolling system and melee attacks.

Brick Breaker

Brick Breaker

A classic arcade-style Brick Breaker game where players control a paddle at the bottom of the screen to bounce a ball and break bricks arranged at the top. The objective is to clear all the bricks without letting the ball fall off the screen.

Brick Breaker Gameplay

Brick Breaker

Features.

1.Classic Gameplay: A traditional Brick Breaker experience where players use a paddle to bounce the ball and destroy bricks.

2.Intuitive Controls: Simple controls to move the paddle left and right, ensuring easy gameplay for all ages.

3.Smooth Physics: Realistic ball and paddle interaction for a satisfying and responsive gaming experience.

4.Increasing Difficulty: Each level becomes progressively harder with more bricks, different layouts, and faster ball speed.

City Runner

City Runner

City Runner is an endless runner game created in Unreal Engine using C++. Players navigate a dynamically generated, curved world while collecting power-ups such as shields, high jumps, and magnets. The game offers an immersive experience with smooth mechanics, engaging environments, and a focus on player skill progression

City Runner Gameplay

City Runner

Features.

1.Smooth Player Movement: Basic movement of slide turn on lane, jump and slide.

2.Shield: Grants:  the temporary invincibility, protecting the player from obstacles and hazards for a limited time.

3.High Jump: Increases the player's jump height, allowing them to reach higher platforms or avoid obstacles more easily.
4.Magnet: Attracts nearby collectibles or items, making it easier for the player to gather them without having to move directly towards them.
